OUR STORY

Cake delight was founded in October 2009. Martha dine and then business partner, Lisa john started by baking for children's birthday parties before realizing how popular cake was with adults too! 15 years later and Martha is the sole owner and the business continues to grow from strength to strength. Everything is hand iced and baked in small batches to really give the essence of simple old fashioned cooking.
